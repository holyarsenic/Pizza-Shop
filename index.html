ABOUT US

Born in Brooklyn, Pizza Moto is a woman-owned catering truck offering wood-fired, pizza-centric Italian-inspired feasts. We lovingly tote our hand-built (and quite patina-ed) oven over the Hudson Valley, Catskills, Brooklyn, and beyond, so we can bring the (pizza) party right to you! Yes, our mobile hearth is the heart of our biz but, in addition to pies, we prepare an array of other scrumptious offerings to complete your festivities, such as: charcuterie and cheese grazing tables, small bites, salads + sides and desserts.

You might call us high/low, in fact, we kinda consider ourselves chameleons of the catering world as we’ll customize a menu to mesh with your venue, type of celebration, personal tastes and budget. We do everything from birthday bashes and baby showers to rehearsal dinners and weddings. Whether your vision for your gathering skews more casual/sassy…or elevated/classy (or, well, somewhere in between!) we’d love to dream up a menu and tailor a service style that fits your vibe. We look forward to cooking for you!
